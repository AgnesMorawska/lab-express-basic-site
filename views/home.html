The Artists's Atelier

For this project we were asked to create a website that speaks about a artist, musician or famous person. There are many talented people in the world but for this project I wanted to take the portunity to share about my mum. She has been painting since I can remember. One of my earliest memories I have is seeing her painting while amonghts the sand dunes on a beach in Stavanger, Norway...

This website is dedicated to her and her passion for art. Welcome and enjoy
